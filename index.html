About me

I am a PhD student at School of Management, Fudan University. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Hong Ling and Prof. Cheng Zhang. I received my bachelor degree of management in information management & information systems from Nanjing University. My research interets concern social network services, e-commerce, and virtual worlds.
